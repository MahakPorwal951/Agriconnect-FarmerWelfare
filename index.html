Objective of this website is to connect farmers across the country, share knowledge,give access to market, disaster relief, Innovation and growth.

Connecting farmers across the country is important to build a strong agriculture system. With the right plans and support, farmers can get the seeds, tools, and money they need. By linking farmers to big stores and restaurants, they can sell more and earn better. Easy-to-use online platforms should also be created, so farmers can quickly register their empty land for farming, helping them grow more crops and use their land better. This will benefit both small farmers and larger businesses.

Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
